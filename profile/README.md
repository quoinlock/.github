## Quion what?

![quoin-typeform](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8fe5e937-852b-4878-87f7-618cb1f939a4)

A 'quoin' ("coin") is a wedge used by typesetters to lock text into a type form so that it remains firmly in place during the printing process.  In modern digital publishing managing Ingram has simplified the distribution of books to nearly ever bookseller on earth... but what small press has the time to track, validate, and manage changes across all the hundreds of bookseller websites?  We can help you keep an eye on the big bad web out there to give you visibility into all your product listings, to put some control back in your hands so that you can focus on publishing great work and supporting your authors.
